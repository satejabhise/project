Why should you have a blog?

A blog is like a website, however on a blog, you keep adding to your content. On a blog, you can also allow comments, where a person can say what they think of your posts. These are little pieces of writing which you can keep adding onto your blog. There are many types of blogs, these include:personal, business, lifestyle, and many more. Having a blog is a good way to express yourself and your opinions through writing. Click on Other tips & tricks for more on how to grow and run your blog.
